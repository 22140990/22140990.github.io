Celtec Games

About Celtec Games

At Celtec Games, we are a passionate and innovative independent game development studio based in North East Wales, UK. We specialise in creating original titles for desktop, mobile and console platforms, such as Ghost Phaser, No Return, Warp Drifters and many more. We create games that entertain and educate audiences of all ages.

At Celtec, we believe that games are more than entertainment — they are a gateway to imagination, learning, and creativity. Our adventures spark creativity in young minds and offer thrilling challenges that keep players on the edge of their seats. Whether it's our platformers, driving games, or FPS (first-person shooter) games, our worlds captivate and inspire.

Our Mission

From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a respected name in the gaming industry, having released many titles since our company's launch. Our journey has brought together a team of dedicated developers, artists, and storytellers with a shared goal of pushing the boundaries of gaming.

Ask anyone at Celtec what the secret to creating games is, and they’ll tell you: it’s all about creativity, vision, technology, and the strong sense of belonging that fuels our team. We’ve reached where we are today by empowering our people and igniting our passion to develop the best cross-platform experiences in the UK — and beyond!

What We Stand For

  • Creativity and Innovation – Every game we create is built with original ideas and unique storylines.
  • Quality and Excellence – We hold the highest standards to ensure each title impresses players.
  • Community and Engagement – Our players are at the heart of what we do. We listen, learn, and evolve our games based on user feedback.
  • Education and Entertainment – We believe games should be both fun and educational, challenging the mind while staying enjoyable.
