About Celtec Games

At Celtec Games, we are a passionate and innovative independent game development studio based in North East Wales, UK. We specialise in creating original titles for desktop, mobile and console platforms, such as Ghost Phaser, No return, Warp Drifters and many more. We create games that will entertain and educate audiences of all ages. At Celtec, we believe that games are more than entertainment, they are a gateway to imagination, learning and creativity. Our adventures spark creativity in young minds and give a thrilling challenge that keeps players on the edge of their seats whether it's our platformers, driving games or FPS (first person shooter) games, our worlds captivate and inspire.

Our Mission

From humble beginnings, we have grown into a respected name in the gaming industry and we have released many titles since our companies launch. Our journey since then has been to bring together a team of dedicated developers, artists, and storytellers who share the common goal of pushing the boundaries of gaming.

Ask anyone at Celtec what the secret to creating games is and they will tell you: it's all about creativity, vision, technology and the strong sense of belonging that fuels our team. We have reached where we are today by empowering our people and igniting our passion to develop the best cross-platform experiences in the country and even the world!

What we stand for

  • Creativity and Innovation - Every game we create is built with original ideas and unique story lines
  • Quality and excellence - We have the highest standards and ensure each title we release impresses players
  • Community and Engagement - Our players are at the heart of what we do. We listen and learn from users based on feedback and use it to evolve our games
  • Education and Entertainment - We believe games should be both fun and educational, so we create games that challenge the mind while staying enjoyable
